  • ✓ Direct gallery links: One tap from notification to their photos — no access code re-entry
  • ✓ Order tracking: Delivery estimates and status updates keep parents informed post-purchase
  • ✓ Reorder prompts: "Picture day photos expire in 5 days" — clear, actionable, not panicky
